Instructions

In this experiment you will see a series of simple sentences, and decide whether each is true or false.

Sentences will appear one at a time in the middle of the screen. If the sentence is true, such as "A robin has a red breast", press "F". If the sentence is false, such as "A robin is a fish", press "J".

Respond as quickly and accurately as possible. Each sentence will remain on the screen until you make a response, followed by a blank screen for 2 seconds, and then the next sentence.

You will have a few 30-second rest periods during the experiment. After each rest period, you will be prompted to resume the experiment by pressing "SPACE".
